Our Story

The Faculty of Humanities & Social Sciences

Established alongside Kapilvastu Multiple Campus in 2019, FOHSS is one of the leading departments in Nepal's Lumbini Province — in student enrollment, range of programs, and community impact.

We've prepared a strategic plan to revitalize humanities and social sciences education, moving forward with a clear vision, mission, and goals — identifying key interventions to serve Kapilvastu and beyond.

Our programs develop quality human resources equipped with critical thinking, research skills, digital literacy, and commitment to Nepal's social development.

Research & Scholarship

Knowledge That Serves Nepal

FOHSS research addresses real challenges — from cultural heritage to social justice and evidence-based policy.

Focus Areas

Active Research Themes

📜 Cultural Heritage Preservation

Documenting Nepal's languages, literature, arts and traditions — with focus on Kapilvastu's unique historical and Buddhist heritage.

⚖️ Social Justice & Equity

Gender equality, poverty, marginalization and inclusive development — producing evidence for policy change at local and national levels.

🌱 Sustainable Rural Development

Climate change, environmental sustainability and rural livelihoods in the Terai — connecting academic knowledge to community resilience.

📊 Policy & Governance Analysis

Examining federalism, local governance and public policy impact on communities in Lumbini Province through qualitative and quantitative methods.

🔢 Quantitative & Mixed Methods

SPSS-based analysis, survey research and mixed-methods design — supported through the KMC Research Toolkit and AI-powered tools.

🤖 AI-Powered Research Tools Available

The 22-module ResearchAI Platform, 18-step SPSS workflow, and APA 7th edition guide — built by our department head for KMC students.

About the Creator

Building Nepal's Digital Academic Future from Kapilvastu

Mr. Bishnu Singh Rayamajhi is a Mathematics Lecturer and the Head of Humanities & Social Sciences at Kapilvastu Multiple Campus, Taulihawa — a constituent campus of Tribhuvan University in Lumbini Province, Nepal.

As the Founder of UCDL Academy, he creates bilingual (Nepali & English) educational content for students across Taulihawa and Kapilvastu — spanning AI, mathematics, data science, and research methodology — delivered through YouTube and the SDROK digital platform.

His work sits at the intersection of mathematics education, research methodology, and educational technology. He has built a comprehensive suite of AI-powered academic tools: the 22-module ResearchAI Platform, 18-step SPSS Toolkit, APA 7th edition guides, Class 11–12 Math website, and this departmental digital hub — all serving KMC students and the broader Nepali academic community.
