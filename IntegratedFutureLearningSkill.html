⚠️ Facilitator Mindset Shift

Traditional Teacher Future Skills Facilitator
"I know everything.""We learn together."
"Listen and take notes.""Do and discover."
"One correct answer.""Many possible solutions."
"I talk, you listen.""You do, I guide."
"Textbook is the source.""Real world is the source."

🎓 Essential Facilitator Skills

  • Wait Time: After asking a question, count to 10 silently. Students need time to think.
  • Redirecting: When a student asks "Is this right?" ask "What do you think?"
  • Managing Noise: Use a bell or clap pattern. Don't shout over students.
  • Handling "I Don't Know": Say "Pretend you do know. What's your best guess?"
  • Praise Effort, Not Talent: Say "You worked hard on that" not "You are smart."

R Essential for NEB project work and competitive exam preparation
T 4 weeks; Week 4 = Final AI project presentation

Session 1: AI or Not? No Tech Needed

  • Show a 3-minute video of AI-generated art vs. human art. Ask: "Which is which?"
  • Distribute 10 cards describing tasks. Groups sort into: AI can do / AI cannot do / AI should not do
  • Discuss: Why did you put each card there? Focus on ethical reasoning
  • Exit Ticket: "One thing AI surprised me with today"

Session 2: My First AI Chat

  • Students open ChatGPT or Gemini (free account)
  • Ask: "Explain [difficult NEB topic] like I'm 15 years old"
  • Try 3 different subjects
  • Notebook Entry: Which explanation was best? Why?

⚠️ Golden Rule

AI is your TUTOR, not your REPLACEMENT. Always add your own thinking!

The C-R-A-T-E Formula

LetterMeaningExample
CContext"I am a Class 11 NEB student..."
RRole"Act as a science teacher..."
AAction"Explain photosynthesis..."
TTone"...in simple, friendly language"
EExamples"Like: Plants eat sunlight..."

Activity: Prompt Olympics

  • Students rewrite 5 weak prompts using C-R-A-T-E
  • Test in real-time and score outputs (1-5 scale)
  • Gallery Walk: Post best prompts, vote on most effective

Session 4: Deepfake Detective

5 Signs of Deepfake:

  1. Unnatural blinking
  2. Lighting mismatch
  3. Audio-lip sync issues
  4. Too smooth/waxy skin
  5. Weird background artifacts

The AI Sandwich Method

🧑 YOUR IDEA → 🤖 AI DRAFT → ✏️ YOUR EDIT → 🎯 YOUR FINAL ↑ ↑ ↑ Human Tool Human (40%) (20%) (40%)

Never submit AI output directly! Always add your own voice.

Activity: AI Tutor Challenge

  • Pick one hard topic from NEB book
  • Ask AI to explain it simply
  • Teach it back to your group using your own words
  • Create 5 practice questions on that topic

Project: AI-Assisted Research Paper

  • Topic: Choose any topic from NEB syllabus
  • Length: 1,000 words
  • Rules: Use AI for sources/outline; Write yourself; Add analysis; Cite sources

🎓 Assessment Tip

Don't grade the AI output. Grade the student's transformation of that output:

  • Personal examples added?
  • Local/Nepali context inserted?
  • Critical questions raised?
  • Proper citations included?

The CRAAP Test for Sources

LetterQuestion
CurrencyIs it recent?
RelevanceDoes it help my topic?
AuthorityWho wrote it? Are they experts?
AccuracyIs there evidence? Other sources agree?
PurposeWhy was this written? (Inform? Sell? Fool?)

Rule: If you answer "No" to 2 or more, DON'T use it!

Research Proposal Structure

  1. TITLE: Clear and specific
  2. RESEARCH QUESTION: What exactly are you trying to find out?
  3. WHY IT MATTERS: Why should anyone care?
  4. LITERATURE REVIEW: What do others already know? (5+ sources)
  5. METHODOLOGY: How will you find answers?
  6. EXPECTED OUTCOMES: What do you think you'll discover?
  7. TIMELINE: When will you do each part?
  8. REFERENCES: Proper citations

Show, Don't Tell

❌ Telling✅ Showing
"She was sad.""Her shoulders dropped. A tear fell on her notebook."
"He was angry.""He slammed the door. His face turned red."
"It was scary.""The wind howled. The lights flickered. No one answered her call."

The 50/30/20 Budget Rule

50% → NEEDS (food, transport, school fees) 30% → WANTS (movies, games, snacks) 20% → SAVINGS (future, emergency)

The Rule of 72

72 ÷ Interest Rate = Years to double your money

At 10% interest: 72 ÷ 10 = 7.2 years to double!
