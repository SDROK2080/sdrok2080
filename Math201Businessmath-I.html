📋 Course Overview

This course introduces mathematical techniques through real-world economic and business examples. You will learn to solve problems in demand/supply, pricing, investment decisions, and profit maximization — plus hands-on Excel skills for each topic. The course bridges abstract math with practical business decision-making.

🎯 Study Tips for BBA Students

📊 Practice Excel alongside theory — every unit has a lab component. Don't skip it!
📈 Draw graphs — visualizing demand, supply & cost curves makes concepts stick.
📝 Solve past questions — focus on application problems, not just theory.
📋 Formula sheet — create your own summary of all key formulas.
🧠 Understand before memorizing — Business math is about applying math to real business decisions. Connect every formula to a real-world scenario.
"Mathematics is not about numbers, equations, computations, or algorithms: it is about understanding."
— William Paul Thurston
