📌 Workshop Overview

A comprehensive professional development program designed for university faculty, researchers, trainers, and academic leaders. This intensive workshop equips participants with practical AI competencies across teaching, research, data analysis, automation, and responsible AI adoption in higher education.

🎓 Certification Requirements

Participants must complete the following to earn certification:

  • Attend at least 90% of all sessions (38 out of 42 hours)
  • Complete all daily practical exercises
  • Submit a comprehensive Prompt Engineering Portfolio
  • Submit one AI-enhanced teaching or research project
  • Present the Capstone Project to the cohort
  • Achieve at least 60% on the overall assessment
Certificate Title: "Advanced AI for Higher Education: Faculty, Research, and Training Professional Development Program (42 Hours)"
