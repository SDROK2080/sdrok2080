Qualitative Research Process Step-by-Step with Free AI Tools
1
Research Problem / Topic Identification
Define the phenomenon, context, and significance
✦ Claude ✦ ChatGPT ✦ Perplexity AI
2
Literature Review & Theoretical Framing
Survey existing studies; establish conceptual framework
✦ Elicit ✦ Connected Papers ✦ NotebookLM ✦ Perplexity AI
3
Research Questions Formulation
Craft open-ended, exploratory questions aligned with the paradigm
✦ Claude ✦ ChatGPT ✦ Google Gemini
4
Research Design Selection
Case Study · Ethnography · Phenomenology · Grounded Theory
✦ Claude ✦ MS Copilot ✦ ChatGPT
5
Sampling Strategy
Purposive · Snowball · Theoretical Sampling
✦ Claude ✦ ChatGPT ✦ Google Gemini
6
Data Collection
Interviews · Focus Groups · Observation · Documents
✦ Otter.ai ✦ Trint ✦ NotebookLM ✦ Claude
7
Data Organization & Transcription
Convert audio/video recordings to structured text
✦ Otter.ai (Free) ✦ Whisper (OpenAI) ✦ NotebookLM
8
Data Analysis
Coding → Categorization → Theme Development
✦ ATLAS.ti ✦ MAXQDA ✦ Claude ✦ NotebookLM
9
Interpretation of Findings
Relate themes to theory, context, and participants' meanings
✦ Claude ✦ ChatGPT ✦ NotebookLM
10
Validation / Trustworthiness
Credibility · Transferability · Dependability · Confirmability
✦ Claude ✦ MAXQDA ✦ ATLAS.ti
11
Conclusion & Report Writing
Synthesize findings; discuss implications and limitations
✦ Claude ✦ ChatGPT ✦ MS Copilot ✦ Google Gemini
🔄 Optional Iteration: Qualitative research is inherently iterative — findings at any stage may prompt you to revisit earlier steps such as refining research questions, expanding sampling, or collecting additional data.

Quantitative Research Process Step-by-Step with Free AI Tools
1
Research Problem Identification
Define a measurable, testable problem within a specific domain
✦ Claude ✦ ChatGPT ✦ Perplexity AI
2
Literature Review
Identify gaps; review empirical studies and prior findings
✦ Elicit ✦ Connected Papers ✦ NotebookLM ✦ Perplexity AI
3
Theoretical Framework
Select theories that explain the relationships between variables
✦ Claude ✦ ChatGPT ✦ NotebookLM
4
Formulation of Hypothesis / Research Questions
State directional or null hypotheses; specify variables
✦ Claude ✦ ChatGPT ✦ Google Gemini
5
Research Design Selection
Experimental · Survey · Correlational · Causal-Comparative
✦ Claude ✦ MS Copilot ✦ ChatGPT
6
Operationalization of Variables
Define independent, dependent, and control variables with measurable indicators
✦ Claude ✦ ChatGPT ✦ DATAtab
7
Sampling Design
Probability Sampling: random, stratified, cluster, systematic
✦ Claude ✦ ChatGPT ✦ DATAtab
8
Data Collection
Questionnaires · Standardized Tests · Instruments · Sensors
✦ Claude (design survey) ✦ Google Gemini ✦ MS Copilot
9
Data Processing & Cleaning
Handle missing values, outliers, coding errors, normalization
✦ Julius AI ✦ Google Colab ✦ ChatGPT (code) ✦ Claude (code)
10
Statistical Analysis
Descriptive → Inferential Statistics (t-test, ANOVA, regression, chi-square)
✦ DATAtab ✦ Julius AI ✦ Obviously AI ✦ Google Colab
11
Results Interpretation
Accept/reject hypotheses; explain statistical significance and effect sizes
✦ DATAtab ✦ Julius AI ✦ Claude ✦ Statology
12
Conclusion & Generalization
Draw inferences; assess external validity and generalizability
✦ Claude ✦ ChatGPT ✦ Google Gemini
13
Report Writing & Publication
APA/MLA formatted manuscript; journal submission or thesis writing
✦ Claude ✦ ChatGPT ✦ MS Copilot ✦ Gamma
