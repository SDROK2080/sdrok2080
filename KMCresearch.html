APA 7th Edition
Reference & Citation Format
📌
APA 7th Edition is the standard for Scopus-indexed journal articles. Use the formats below for in-text citations and reference list entries.
Source TypeIn-text CitationReference List Format
Journal Article(Author, Year)Author, A. A. (Year). Title. Journal Name, Volume(Issue), pages. https://doi.org/xxxxx
Book(Author, Year, p. xx)Author, A. A. (Year). Book title. Publisher.
Edited Book Chapter(Author, Year)Author, A. A. (Year). Chapter title. In E. Editor (Ed.), Book title (pp. xx–xx). Publisher.
Thesis/Dissertation(Author, Year)Author, A. A. (Year). Title [Doctoral dissertation/Master's thesis, Institution]. Repository name. URL
Website(Organization, Year)Organization. (Year, Month Day). Title of page. Site Name. URL
Government Report(Agency, Year)Agency Name. (Year). Report title. Publisher. URL
✏️
Key APA 7th rules: DOI as hyperlink. No place of publication for books. Up to 20 authors before "…". Running head removed (institutional papers only). Use hanging indent for references.

🧠 Humanizer Techniques for Academic Writing:
1
Vary sentence length: Mix short punchy sentences with longer compound-complex ones. Avoid all sentences being the same length — a hallmark of AI output.
2
Add first-person scholarly voice: Phrases like "The present study argues…", "The researcher observes…", "Contrary to expectation…" add human scholarly voice.
3
Insert hedging language: Use "may suggest", "appears to indicate", "it is plausible that" rather than absolute declarative statements.
4
Cite abundantly: AI detectors lower their score when text has dense, varied academic citations integrated naturally into sentences.
5
Use context-specific examples: Reference Nepal, Rupandehi District, Kapilvastu, your student population. AI text is generic — specificity humanizes it.
6
Restructure paragraphs: Move topic sentences, split or merge paragraphs, change paragraph order. Structural changes defeat most AI pattern detectors.
